## TENANT OBLIGATIONS

The Tenant agrees to:

1. Operate the business in a lawful and professional manner
2. Maintain all required business licenses and insurance
3. Keep the premises in good repair and condition
4. Pay all utilities and services related to the premises
5. Maintain comprehensive general liability insurance
6. Not assign or sublease without written permission
7. Comply with all environmental regulations

---

## LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS

The Landlord agrees to:

1. Provide the premises in suitable condition for commercial use
2. Maintain the structural integrity of the building
3. Ensure common areas are properly maintained
4. Provide reasonable access to the premises
5. Pay property taxes and building insurance (unless otherwise specified)

---

## LATE PAYMENT AND DEFAULT

If rent is not received by the 5th of the month, a late fee of 5% of the monthly rent will be assessed. Continued non-payment constitutes default and may result in eviction and legal action to recover unpaid amounts.

---

## TERMINATION AND RENEWAL

**Notice Period**: Either party must provide at least 60 days written notice before the expiration date to terminate or renegotiate this lease.

**Renewal**: This lease may be renewed by mutual written agreement. Renewal terms, including rent adjustments, shall be negotiated prior to the expiration date.

---

## INDEMNIFICATION

Tenant agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Landlord from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from Tenant's use of the premises, except for those caused by Landlord's gross negligence or willful misconduct.
