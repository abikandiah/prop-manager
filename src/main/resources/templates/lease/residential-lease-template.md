## TENANT RESPONSIBILITIES

The Tenant agrees to:

1. Maintain the premises in clean and habitable condition
2. Pay rent on time each month
3. Notify the Landlord promptly of any needed repairs
4. Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
5. Not make alterations without written permission
6. Allow reasonable access for inspections and repairs

---

## LANDLORD RESPONSIBILITIES

The Landlord agrees to:

1. Provide habitable premises that meet all safety codes
2. Make necessary repairs in a timely manner
3. Respect tenant privacy with proper notice for entry
4. Maintain common areas and building systems

**Maintenance**: {{maintenance_responsibility}}

---

## LATE PAYMENT

If rent is not received by the 5th of the month, a late fee of $50.00 will be assessed. Continued late payment may result in eviction proceedings.

---

## TERMINATION AND NOTICE

Either party may terminate this lease by providing written notice at least 60 days before the intended termination date. Early termination without proper notice may result in financial penalties.
